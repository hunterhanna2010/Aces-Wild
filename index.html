aces wild

Aces Wild features two players, Player Z and Player M. The game starts and the cards flip continuously to create one stack of cards.
Players watch for Aces. The first player to slap the stack with a keystroke of "Z" or "M" wins the round and 1 point. If a player slaps on a card that is not an Ace, that player loses the round and 1 point. Rounds continue until there are no more cards left to be dealt.
